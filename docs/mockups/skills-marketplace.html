Private Equity

Extract key financials, deal terms, and company metrics from Confidential Information Memorandums.

What it does

Automatically extracts structured data from PDF CIMs including revenue, EBITDA, asking price, growth rates, customer concentration, and key deal terms.

Sample prompts

"Parse this CIM and extract the financials"
"What's the EBITDA and asking price for this deal?"

  "revenue": 12500000,
  "ebitda": 2200000,
  "asking_price": 8000000,
  "ebitda_margin": 0.176
